Instructions

  • The selection of ships position begins with the selection of ship1 position, and continues automatically to ship2 and ship3.
  • the position of your ships can only be horizontal or vertical
  • if you choose to position your ship horizontally, you can position your ship only in one direction right or left. same applies when positioning your ship vertically (up/down).
  • ship sizes:
    • Ship 1: 3 squares
    • Ship 2: 2 squares
    • Ship 3: 3 squares
